Maison Lumière

Sample host page used to verify the reassurance toast against a typical hospitality background. The default config shows Booking, Google and Tripadvisor side-by-side; pass ?id=YOUR_HOTEL_ID on the script tag to load a published config from configs/<id>.json.

Embed by adding <script async src="./widget.js"></script> on your page — the widget auto-mounts a fixed bottom-left toast with a slide-in reveal.
